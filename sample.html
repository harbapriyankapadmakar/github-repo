Chocolate chips cookies

These super soft and chewy chocolate chip cookies are the most popular cookie recipe on my website. Melted butter, more brown sugar than white sugar,

Ingredients:
  • 2 cup flour
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven 375 C
  2. Mix dry ingredients
  3. Add wet ingredients
  4. Fold in chocolate chips
  5. Bake for 12 minutes
